**Solving the maze**

We start at the origin (0,0) and follow the path until we reach the target (3,4).

1. Start at (0,0).
2. Move to (1,0).
3. Move to (1,1).
4. Move to (2,1).
5. Move to (3,1).
6. Move to (3,2).
7. Move to (2,2).
8. Move to (1,2).
9. Move to (1,3).
10. Move to (2,3).
11. Move to (2,4).
12. Move to (3,4).
